Hardware Hacking Village at Seasides Conference

HexGuard is a group of security engineers who conduct and manage the Hardware Hacking Village hw101.me at conferences around India and across the globe. We are part of Seasides conference, bringing hands-on hardware security education and community to security enthusiasts.

About HexGuard

Building hardware security community through hands-on education and training

Community Driven

Global Presence

Expert Team
